Welcome!

Come join us at your new duty station here in JBLM.

If you are looking to save money while you wait for your household goods to arrive stop in and see us! We have two large buildings full of merchandise. New items arrive daily from consignors and donations.

Donations

Do you have unwanted household goods or clothing you no longer need? Why not donate? The Thrift Shop may be what you are looking for! All profits from the sale of donated items go to support our military community and welfare programs through the JBLM community.
If you're interested in our welfare program, complete the welfare packet and contact us.

Consignment

Would you like to be paid for cleaning out your house? Bring in your gently used items to sell at the Thrift Shop!
You can consign up to 16 items per day. Our fee is 35%. Consignments are accepted from 10am - 12pm on Thursday's only. Please call on Tuesday to make appointment.
Building 2071, last door on the right.

PCS'ing?

Leaving within the next 90 days? Do you need to lighten your load? Would you like to earn money for those items you will no longer need or want? And all those great flea market finds you can’t take back?With your orders, you can consign up to 40 items per day. Stop into the Thrift Shop to open an account. Pick up an item list form to fill out at the comfort of your own home. Bring in all items, paperwork, orders and 3 self-addressed stamped envelopes so your check can be sent after you leave.

What we don't accept:

Air conditioners, bathtubs, old baby cribs, children's car seats, computers, printers, copy machines, damaged, broken, ripped or soiled furniture, dirty BBQ grills, dirty rugs or carpets, dishwashers, electric or kerosene heaters, flourescent lightbulbs, mattresses, box springs, metal spring beds, paint, batteries, flammable items, poisonous/hazardous chemicals, or office dividers.

Volunteer

Become a Thrift Shop Volunteer!
Complete the Volunteer Packet and turn it in to a Thrift Shop Volunteer, Coordinator, or Store Manager.
You’ll have the following benefits:

  • Paid childcare provided
  • Pick the hours you work
  • Special consignnor benefits: up to 40 items & up to 80%
  • A yearly holiday volunteer appreciation luncheon
  • Complimentary snacks are provided
  • Have fun & meet new people!
