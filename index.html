Welcome!

Come join us at your new duty station here in JBLM.

If you are looking to save money while you wait for your household goods to arrive stop in and see us! We have two large buildings full of merchandise. New items arrive daily from consignors and donations.

Donations

Do you have unwanted household goods & clothing you no longer need? Why not donate? The Thrift Shop may be what you are looking for! All profits from the sale of donated items go to support our military community and welfare programs through the JBLM community. If you're interested in our welfare program, download the welfare packet and contact us.

Consignment

Would you like to be paid for cleaning out your house? Bring in your unwanted or gently used items to sell at the Thrift Shop!
You can consign up to 16 items per day. Our fee is 35% Consignments are accepted on Thursday's only (Must call on Tuesday to make appointment.) 10am-12pm in Bldg. 2071 (last door to the right of Bldg.)

PCS'ing?

Leaving within the next 90 days? Do you need to lighten your load? Would you like to earn money for those items you will no longer need or want? And all those great flea market finds you can’t take back?With your orders, you can consign up to 40 items per day. Stop into the Thrift Shop to open an account. Pick up an item list form to fill out at the comfort of your own home. Bring in all items, paperwork, orders and 3 self-addressed stamped (business size) envelopes so your check can be sent after you leave.

What we don't accept:

Air conditioners, bathtubs, old baby cribs, children's car seats, computers, printers, copy machines, damaged, broken, ripped or soiled furniture, dirty BBQ grills, dirty rugs or carpets, dishwashers, electric or kerosene heaters, flourescent lightbulbs, mattresses, box springs, metal spring beds, paint, batteries, flammable items, poisonous/hazardous chemicals, or office dividers.

Volunteer

Become a Thrift Shop Volunteer!

You’ll have the following benefits:

  • Paid childcare provided
  • Pick the hours you work

  • Provide FUN & meet new people!
